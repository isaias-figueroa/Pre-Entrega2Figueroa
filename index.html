THE WEEKND

The Weeknd is the stage name of Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He was born on February 16, 1990, in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The Weeknd first gained significant attention with his mixtapes released in 2010, which were characterized by his unique blend of R&B, pop, and electronic music, often with dark and moody themes.His enigmatic persona and distinct falsetto vocals contributed to his rapid rise in popularity, and he soon garnered a dedicated fanbase. In 2011, he released his first studio album, "House of Balloons," which received critical acclaim and helped establish him as a significant figure in contemporary music.

The Weeknd's music often explores themes of love, sex, drugs, and partying, with an underlying sense of emotional vulnerability and introspection. His sound evolved over the years, incorporating elements of various genres, including synth-pop, funk, and even a bit of rock.
